Me & Roboco by Shuhei Miyazaki

This one's fairly new to Jump and it's definitly special!
Me and Roboco is a gag manga that is essentially built upon references to other notable Shonen Jump series. In my opinion, the jokes land pretty well, and it's the perfect type of series to pair with other, more serious, weekly reads.

A character poll hasn't been launched yet, but if I were to rank the characters, my list would look a little like this:

  1. ROBOCO!!
  2. Motsuo and Gaji Gorrilia
  3. Mom
  4. Bondo
  5. roboco's knees
  6. Nyonta
